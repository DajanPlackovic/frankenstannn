Igorrr and the Doctor

For centuries, a great injustice has been done to Igorrr, his contribution to Adam's creation minimized and neglected, all while Frankenstannn's name still lives on the lips of all. Igorrr was not even mentioned in the most famous reports of Frankenstannn's life, that written by Mary Shelley, a grave injustice indeed.

Today, we right that wrong. We, Frankenstannn, meet monthly in the pubs of Dublin to celebrate the genius that is Igorrr. Join us in venerating the hunchbacked desert artist if you feel compelled to redress the wrong done to his name, or else if you just like having a beer while listening to a fascinating blend of electronica and metal. That works too.
